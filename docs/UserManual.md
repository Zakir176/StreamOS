## Getting Started

### First Time Setup

When you open StreamOS, you'll see the **Profile Selection Screen**. This is your entry point to the platform.

1. **Select a Profile**: Choose an existing profile by clicking on the profile avatar or name
2. **Create a Profile** (if none exist): Click the "+" button to create your first profile
   - Enter a username
   - Choose an age category: **Kids**, **Teen**, or **Adult**
   - Confirm to create the profile

### How Profiles Work

StreamOS uses profiles to personalize your experience and manage content access:

- **Kids Profile**: Access only family-friendly content
- **Teen Profile**: Access kids and teen-appropriate content
- **Adult Profile**: Access all content in your library

Each profile:
- Has its own watch history and resume positions
- Can have a custom avatar
- Respects age-based content filtering
- Has a separate theme/display preference

## Profiles

### Creating a New Profile

1. Go to **Settings** (from the navigation menu in the top right)
2. Click **"Add Profile"** or the **"+" button**
3. Enter a profile name
4. Select an age category (Kids / Teen / Adult)
5. Click **Save**

The new profile is now ready to use and will appear on the home screen.

### Switching Profiles

**From the Home Screen:**
- Click on a different profile avatar to switch immediately

**From Within the App:**
- Click the profile dropdown in the top right corner
- Select **"Switch Profile"** and choose another profile
- Click the new profile to switch

### Editing a Profile

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Find the profile you want to edit
3. Click the **edit icon** next to the profile name
4. Update the profile name or age category
5. Click **Save**

### Deleting a Profile

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click the **trash icon** next to the profile you want to delete
3. Confirm the deletion

⚠️ **Note**: Deleting a profile will also delete its watch history and resume positions.

## Browsing Your Library

### Library Layout

The **Library** view is organized into content sections:

- **Movies**: Stand-alone films
- **TV Shows**: Multi-season television series
- **Anime**: Anime series and movies
- **Continue Watching**: Partially watched content (see [Continue Watching](#continue-watching))

### Finding Content

**Browse by Category:**
1. Open the **Library**
2. Scroll horizontally through each content section
3. Click on any title to view more details

**Search (if available):**
- Use the search bar to find specific titles by name

### Content Information

When you hover over or click on a title, you'll see:
- **Poster/Thumbnail**: Visual cover art
- **Title**: Name of the movie, show, or episode
- **Description**: Plot summary and details
- **Rating Info**: Age category and other metadata
- **Progress Bar** (if partially watched): Shows how much you've watched

### Content Filtering

Content is automatically filtered based on your profile's age category:
- **Kids Profile**: Only kids content visible
- **Teen Profile**: Kids and teen content visible
- **Adult Profile**: All content visible

## Watching Videos

### Starting Playback

1. **Find the content** you want to watch in the Library
2. **Click the title** to open the video player
3. **Playback starts automatically**, or click the play button to begin

### Video Player Controls

The player features simple, intuitive controls:

| Control | Function |
|---------|----------|
| **Play/Pause** | Start or pause playback |
| **Volume** | Adjust audio level |
| **Progress Bar** | Seek to any point in the video |
| **Timeline Preview** | Hover over the progress bar to see preview thumbnails |
| **Back Button** | Return to the previous screen (Library or Series view) |

### Keyboard Shortcuts (if enabled)

- **Space** or **P**: Play/Pause
- **M**: Mute/Unmute
- **F**: Fullscreen
- **Esc**: Exit fullscreen or return to library
- **← →**: Rewind/Forward 10 seconds
- **↑ ↓**: Increase/Decrease volume

### Watching Series

When watching a TV show or anime:

1. Select an **episode** from the series view
2. After an episode finishes, **"Next Episode" automatically appears** (if available)
3. Click **"Play Next"** to continue to the next episode
4. Or click **back** to return to the series and pick a different episode

## Continue Watching

The **Continue Watching** section shows all content you've started but not finished.

### How It Works

- Whenever you watch a video and stop before it's finished, it's added to Continue Watching
- Your **resume position is saved automatically**
- When you return to the same video, it starts at your last position
- Videos appear in order of most recently watched

### Removing from Continue Watching

When you finish watching a video completely, it's automatically removed from Continue Watching.

To manually remove a video:
1. Hover over the title in Continue Watching
2. Click the **remove/X button** (if available)
3. The title will no longer appear in this section

## Settings & Configuration

### Accessing Settings

1. Click the **profile dropdown** in the top right corner
2. Select **"Settings"**

### Available Settings

#### Profile Management
- **View All Profiles**: See all profiles on your system
- **Add Profile**: Create a new profile
- **Edit Profile**: Change profile name or age category
- **Delete Profile**: Remove a profile and its data

#### Display Settings
- **Theme**: Choose between different UI themes (Midnight, Cinematic, etc.)
- **Update Avatar**: Change your profile's display picture

#### Library Settings
- **Scan Library**: Manually trigger a scan of your media directory to refresh content
- **Offline Mode**: Enable to disable automatic metadata updates from TMDB
- **API Key**: (Advanced) Configure TMDB API key for metadata enrichment

#### System Settings
- **Media Directory**: Location where StreamOS scans for video files
- **Reset All Data**: Clear all profiles and watch history (use with caution)

## Tips & Tricks

### 1. Keep Your Library Fresh
- Regularly add new videos to your media directory
- Use **"Scan Library"** in Settings to refresh content
- StreamOS will automatically detect new files

### 2. Organize Your Files
- Organize movies and TV shows in separate folders for easier browsing
- Use proper naming conventions:
  - Movies: `Title (Year).mkv`
  - TV Shows: `Show Name - S01E01 - Title.mkv`

### 3. Use Multiple Profiles
- Create different profiles for different family members
- Set age-appropriate categories to protect younger viewers
- Each profile has separate watch history

### 4. Metadata & Thumbnails
- StreamOS automatically fetches metadata from TMDB
- Thumbnails are generated automatically for better browsing
- For better results, use standard naming conventions for your files

### 5. Fullscreen Viewing
- Use fullscreen mode for the best viewing experience
- Hide UI elements for distraction-free watching
- Press Esc to exit fullscreen

### 6. Continue Watching Management
- Your resume position is saved across sessions
- Perfect for long movies and series
- Manually remove finished content to keep the list clean

## Troubleshooting

### Videos Not Appearing in Library

**Problem**: I added videos to the media folder, but they don't show up.

**Solutions**:
1. Go to **Settings → Scan Library** to manually refresh
2. Check that video files are in the correct media directory
3. Ensure files use supported formats (MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV)
4. Verify your profile's age category allows the content

### Thumbnails Not Loading

**Problem**: Video covers or thumbnails aren't displaying.

**Solutions**:
1. Go to **Settings → Scan Library** to regenerate thumbnails
2. Check internet connection if using TMDB metadata
3. Restart StreamOS
4. Clear browser cache and refresh

### Resume Position Not Working

**Problem**: Videos keep playing from the beginning instead of resuming.

**Solutions**:
1. Ensure cookies/localStorage are enabled in your browser
2. Try a different browser
3. Clear browser cache and localStorage
4. Restart the application

### Can't Switch Profiles

**Problem**: Profile switching isn't working.

**Solutions**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Ensure you have multiple profiles created
4. Try logging in again

### Content Not Filtering by Age

**Problem**: I see content that shouldn't be visible for my profile.

**Solutions**:
1. Check your profile's age category in **Settings**
2. Verify the content is tagged with the correct age category in the system
3. Scan library to refresh metadata
4. Contact your system administrator

### Video Playback Issues

**Problem**: Videos stutter, pause, or won't play.

**Solutions**:
1. Check your network connection
2. Try a different video to isolate the issue
3. Reduce video quality if available
4. Close other applications using bandwidth
5. Restart StreamOS and your browser

### Performance Is Slow

**Problem**: The app feels laggy or slow.

**Solutions**:
1. Close unused browser tabs
2. Clear browser cache
3. Reduce number of videos shown at once (reduce scrolling)
4. Restart your browser
5. Check available storage space on your device
